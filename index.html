Welcome to Save the Tigers!

Big Reasons Big Cats Don't Belong in the Barnum and Bailey Circus:

  • Large tigers are transported long distances in very small cages.
  • Cubs are separated from their mother's too early causing emotional distress.
  • Social and physiological needs are denied as tigers are solitary creatures and made to live in unnatural groups.
  • Tigers are trained using food deprivation and punishment.
  • Tiger's are innately afraid of fire and are made to jump through burning hoops.
  • Due to big cats in captivity, 126 captive cats and 23 humans have DIED in the last 25 years.
